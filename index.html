MetroBaz

This app allows the user to display the location of the free Metro Link Nihonbashi, Metro Link Nihonbashi E-Line Buses and the Marounouchi and Hibiya Shuttle buses of Tokyo.

In addition to the routes and the bus locations, MetroBaz provides bus time table at every bus stops and the nearest bus stop from the user location in an intuitive user interface. Whether you’re a resident or a visitor, MetroBaz provides the information you need in a simple, easy-to-use interface.

MetroBaz does not require an account, registration, or subscription.

The app provides the following functionality:

  • View live locations of Metro Buses on the map.
  • Supports 4 different bus lines, Nihonbashi, Ningyocho, Marunouchi and Hibiya..
  • Browse all bus stops and their timetables.
  • Displays the current location of all buses of the selected line.
  • Find the nearest bus stop from your current location.
  • Calculate and display the shortest walking path to the nearest bus stop.
  • Choose between Standard, Hybrid, and Satellite maps.
  • Available in English and Japanese.
